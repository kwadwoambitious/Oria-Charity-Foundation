Why Volunteer with Us?

Join hands with Oria Charity Foundation to make a positive impact on the lives of those in need. Our volunteers are the heart and soul of our organization, and we welcome individuals like you who are passionate about making a difference.

Meaningful Impact

Every task, no matter how small, contributes to our mission of raising funds, providing resources, and conducting initiatives to support those in need. Whether you're feeding the hungry, mentoring a child, your efforts matter.

Personal Growth

Volunteering is an opportunity to learn new skills, gain experience, and challenge yourself. It's a chance to grow both personally and professionally while making a positive impact on the world.

Build Connections

Join a diverse group of individuals who share your commitment to social change. Forge lasting friendships and expand your network while working towards a common goal.

Who Are We?

Our foundation is a non-governmental organization(NGO) established to address specific social, humanitarian, or environmental issues by raising funds, providing resources, and conducting initiatives to support those in need.
